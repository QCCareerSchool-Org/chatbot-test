## How long does it take to grade my assignment?

### Question Variations
- How long does it take to grade my assignment?
- When will my assignment be graded?
- How long until I receive my feedback?

Please note that assignments for the next unit will only become available after you pass your current unit.

## How to Upload Your Assignment Video to YouTube

### Question Variations
- How do I upload my assignment video to YouTube?
- How do I submit a video assignment?
- How do I make a YouTube link for my assignment?

### Answer
Some assignments require you to upload a video to YouTube and submit the link.

1. Sign in to your Google account.
2. Open YouTube and click **Create → Upload Video**.
3. Select your video file.
4. Enter a title for your video.
5. Set the visibility to **Unlisted**.
6. Copy the video link.
7. Paste the link into your assignment submission.

Using the **Unlisted** setting ensures that only people with the link can view your video.

### Escalation
If the student has trouble uploading their video or generating a shareable link:

## How to Compress Images and Add Them to a Word Document

### Question Variations
- How do I compress images for my assignment?
- How do I reduce photo file size?
- How do I insert photos into a Word document?
- My assignment file is too large.

### Answer
If your assignment includes photos, compressing them can reduce file size and make uploading easier.

#### Compress Your Images
1. Go to https://compressjpeg.com/
2. Click **Upload Files**.
3. Select your images.
4. Download the compressed versions.

#### Add Images to a Word Document
1. Open a blank Word document.
2. Click **Insert → Pictures → This Device**.
3. Select your compressed images.
4. Repeat until all images are inserted.

You can then save the document as a PDF before uploading it to your assignment.

### Escalation
If the student continues to have trouble resizing images or uploading assignment files:
- 1-833-600-3751
